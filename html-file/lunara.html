HERO

LUNARA

Lunara is a gentle and compassionate druidess who has spent her life attuned to the moon's energy. She possesses the ability to communicate with animals and manipulate plant life. Growing up in the forest, Lunara developed a profound understanding of nature's balance. Her calming presence and healing powers serve as a guiding light for her companions.

HERO

EMBERA

Embera is a courageous and impulsive fire mage. Growing up near a dormant volcano, she developed a deep affinity for flames and their destructive power. As the daughter of a blacksmith, she learned to manipulate fire with ease. Embera's unyielding spirit and unwavering determination make her a natural leader among the group.

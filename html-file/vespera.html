ENEMY

VESPERA

Vespera, a mysterious and malevolent sorceress, draws her strength from the shadows. Once an outcast with a tragic past, she was seduced by the promises of power offered by the Fleshmancer. Vespera's abilities allow her to manipulate darkness and shadows, striking fear into the hearts of her enemies. Driven by her desire for revenge and a thirst for power, she aligns herself with the Fleshmancer to further her own twisted agenda.

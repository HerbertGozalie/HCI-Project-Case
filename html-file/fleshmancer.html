ENEMY

FLESHMANCER

The Fleshmancer, once a talented alchemist named Arlo Vanthorne, delved into forbidden dark arts in his relentless pursuit of immortality. Consumed by his obsession, he conducted gruesome experiments, melding flesh and magic to create twisted abominations. The Fleshmancer seeks to amass power and unravel the secrets of life and death, using his dark creations and forbidden knowledge to spread chaos and destruction throughout the world.
